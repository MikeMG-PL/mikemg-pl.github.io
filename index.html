My blog is a space where I share my experience and struggles in
game and engine programming.

  • C++ – things that I learned and that fascinate me.

Production & Creative Directing

I also present my opinions on topics related to game production, directing, or managing gamedev projects. I was pleased to lead production of a few small games – awarded locally, and in Europe. I am also a president of a gamedev study circle at Lodz University of Technology, Poland.

  • My design philosophy – you will read about multilayered approach to game production or seamless game tutorials a lot.
  • How to reconcile art and programming teams – and how we assemble all the game pieces together.
  • Student gamedev community – I write about how “Digital Night Studio” gamedev club works and how the students can hop into the game industry. You will also read about an amazing regular event – the ZTGK gamedev contest.
